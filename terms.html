Commercial terms

Commercial points presented more clearly, with fewer hidden assumptions.

Use this page as a quick reference before sending a quotation request or discussing shipment terms.

Quotation basis

MOQ-based pricing with FOB Qingdao as the default quotation basis shown on the source site.

Other shipping terms

CNF, CIF, DDU, and DDP can be discussed depending on the order structure and destination requirements.

Small or special orders

PayPal can be used for small or special orders when appropriate.

Volume discussion

Order-size note

The original site states that orders above 10,000 pieces may qualify for up to 5% discount.

Final pricing should still be confirmed against the actual style mix, construction complexity, order size, and final commercial agreement.

Useful quotation inputs

What to include in the request

  • Style reference, product image, or tech pack
  • Target quantity and size breakdown if available
  • Target delivery window or ship timing
  • Destination market and requested incoterm
  • Any labeling, packaging, or compliance notes already known

Important clarifications

These notes help keep the commercial discussion realistic and consistent.

Pricing

Timing
