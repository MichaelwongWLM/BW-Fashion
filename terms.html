Quotation and shipping points presented more directly.

This page is a practical reference before quotation or export discussion, focusing on the information that usually matters first.

1

Quotation basis

Pricing discussion usually depends on the selected style, material direction, quantity, packing requirement and delivery timing.

2

Shipping basis

Shipping terms can be discussed according to the destination market, order structure and the basis required by the buyer.

3

Useful first inputs

Style reference, target quantity, delivery window and destination market help make early quotation discussion more specific.

Before asking for price

What to prepare for a better quotation request.

  • Reference image, style number or tech pack.
  • Target quantity or approximate volume.
  • Expected delivery timing or sales season.
  • Destination country and any requirement notes.
  • Packing, labeling or special handling details when relevant.
