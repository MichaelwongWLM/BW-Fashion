Commercial terms

Commercial points presented more clearly

Use this page as a quick reference before sending a quotation request or discussing shipment terms.

Quotation basis

MOQ-based pricing with FOB Qingdao as the default quotation basis.

Other shipping terms

CNF, CIF, DDU, and DDP can be discussed depending on the order structure and destination requirements.

Special arrangements

Order structure, payment method, and special handling points can be discussed case by case during quotation.

Volume discussion

Order-size note

Large-quantity orders can be reviewed separately during quotation. Final pricing, lead time, and any volume adjustment should be confirmed in writing.

Useful quotation inputs

What to include in the request

  • Style reference, product image, or tech pack
  • Target quantity and size breakdown if available
  • Requested shipping basis
  • Target delivery window
  • Destination market and key packaging requirements
