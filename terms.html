Commercial terms

Commercial information presented more clearly.

Use this page as a quick reference before starting a quotation or shipment discussion.

Quotation basis

MOQ-based pricing with FOB Qingdao as the default quotation basis.

Other shipping terms

CNF, CIF, DDU and DDP can be discussed depending on order structure and destination requirements.

Small or special orders

PayPal can be used for small or special orders when appropriate.

Volume incentive

Orders above 10,000 pieces may qualify for up to 5% discount.

Final pricing remains subject to actual style mix, construction complexity and final order confirmation.

Recommended quotation inputs

  • Style reference, image or tech pack.
  • Target quantity and delivery window.
  • Destination market and shipping basis required.
  • Any special packing, labeling or compliance notes.
