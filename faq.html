Frequently asked questions

Common buyer questions

Quick answers to the most common product, quotation, shipping, and sampling questions.

What should I include in the first inquiry?

Include a style reference or image, the product category, expected quantity, target delivery window, destination market, and any key fabric, sizing, packaging, or labeling notes.

What product groups are shown on the website?

The current website is organized around outerwear, denim and bottoms, womenswear, pet apparel and accessories, and selected stage-costume references.

Can I ask for FOB Qingdao pricing?

Yes. The commercial terms page presents MOQ-based pricing with FOB Qingdao as the default quotation basis.

Are other shipping terms possible?

Yes. CNF, CIF, DDU, and DDP can be discussed depending on order structure and destination requirements.

How does the contact page work without a backend form?

The contact page generates a structured email draft and also lets buyers copy the inquiry text. This avoids a broken form and keeps the static website lightweight.
