Product references

Grouped product references with direct quotation and sampling entry points.

Each product card now links to the contact page with style and category information prefilled. This makes the move from browsing to inquiry faster and more accurate.

How to use this page

Review the category, choose a reference, then use the product card button to start a more structured discussion.

For quotation

  • Select the closest style reference
  • Add quantity and delivery timing
  • Specify the destination market and requested shipping basis

For sampling

  • Share sample comments clearly
  • Add material or workmanship direction if already known
  • Use the prefilled form to keep the style reference attached

Product names on this page are practical reference labels based on the provided images. Final technical naming and specifications should be confirmed during quotation or sampling discussion.
