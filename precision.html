Sampling & QA

Sampling & QA support

Typical working sequence

1

Inquiry review

Confirm the style direction, target market, expected quantity and target delivery timing.

2

Sample development

Check measurements, materials, workmanship points and buyer comments against the target style.

3

Pre-production check

Confirm the approved reference, labeling, packing notes and production checkpoints before bulk starts.

4

Inspection & delivery

Review quality points, shipment readiness and timing alignment before dispatch.

Typical buyer inputs

  • Style image, tech pack or nearest reference on the site.
  • Expected quantity or development purpose.
  • Measurements, comments or known material preferences.
  • Target delivery window and destination market.

Typical follow-up items

  • Construction and workmanship details against the approved direction.
  • Sample comments and revision points before bulk commitment.
  • Labeling, packing and pre-production checkpoint alignment.
  • Shipment timing and readiness before final dispatch discussion.
