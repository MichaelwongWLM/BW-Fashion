Sampling & QA

Sampling and quality-control support

Typical working sequence

1

Inquiry & reference review

Confirm the style direction, target market, estimated quantity and delivery timing before development starts.

2

Sample development

Check construction, measurements, fabric direction, workmanship points and buyer comments against the target style.

3

Pre-production confirmation

Lock the approved reference, labeling, packing notes and any production checkpoints before bulk starts.

4

Inspection & delivery follow-up

Review quality checkpoints, shipping readiness and timing alignment before dispatch.

What buyers usually provide

  • Style images, tech pack or the nearest reference on the site.
  • Expected quantity or development purpose.
  • Target measurements, comments or known material preferences.
  • Required delivery window and destination market.

What is usually checked during follow-up

  • Construction and workmanship details against the approved direction.
  • Sample comments and revision points before bulk commitment.
  • Pre-production alignment for labels, packing and quality checkpoints.
  • Shipment timing and readiness before final dispatch discussion.
