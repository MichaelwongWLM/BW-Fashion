About the company

Export-oriented garment supply arranged in a clear business format

Qingdao Best-Integrity Trade Co., Ltd. is based in Qingdao, China and focuses on garment production support and export coordination for practical commercial orders. The company works around category review, sample development, production follow-up and shipment preparation so buyers can move from visual reference to discussion more efficiently.

Company profile

The current business presentation centers on apparel categories that can be reviewed quickly by overseas buyers.

  • Main direction includes outerwear, padded jackets, vests, denim bottoms and selected womenswear.
  • Supplementary categories include stage costumes, pet clothing and selected pet accessories.
  • Sampling discussion, production checkpoints and contact details are separated into dedicated pages for clearer communication.
  • Representative style photos are shown as visual references for quotation and sample discussion.

How buyers usually work with us

Review the category

Start from the closest product family and identify the nearest style reference.

Discuss sampling needs

Use the Sampling & QA page to align comments, measurements, materials and checkpoints.

Move into quotation and shipment discussion

Confirm quantity, destination market, delivery timing and required shipping basis.

Why this site is arranged this way

The website is built to help a buyer move through a normal discussion order: first review the style direction, then sample and quality points, then quotation and contact details.
