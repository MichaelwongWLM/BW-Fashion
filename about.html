About the company

Focused apparel sourcing and export support from Qingdao.

Best-Integrity presents a garment-oriented export profile with emphasis on outerwear, workwear, casualwear, denim, pet products and selected performance styles.

Business profile

The site is structured for buyers who want a quick view of product direction, commercial basis and the next step toward sampling or quotation.

  • Core visual range includes jackets, padded outerwear, vests, bombers and related practical styles.
  • Additional categories shown on the site include denim bottoms, tailored womenswear, pet clothing and pet accessories.
  • Selected performance-style visuals are also included in the current product set.
  • Inquiry flow is designed to collect the key information needed for faster first replies.

Product presentation

Category-based browsing helps buyers identify the relevant style family more quickly.

Working approach

Inquiry handling, sampling review, quality checkpoints and delivery discussion are treated as connected steps.

Documentation

For a detailed quotation, buyers are encouraged to share style references, quantity, delivery timing and any technical requirements.

Recommended communication flow

A simple structure for moving from first contact to order discussion.

1

Share style direction

Send images, tech pack or reference numbers to define the product family.

2

Confirm commercial basis

Discuss quantity, target market and delivery window so the quotation basis is clear.

3

Proceed to sampling

Use the Sampling & QA page to align checkpoints and sample review expectations.

4

Move into production

Align inspection points and shipment terms before final order confirmation.
