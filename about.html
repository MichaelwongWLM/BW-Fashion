About the Company

About Qingdao Best-Integrity Trade Co., Ltd.

Qingdao Best-Integrity Trade Co., Ltd. is a Qingdao-based garment export company serving international buyers across multiple apparel categories. We support product sourcing, sampling, quotation, order follow-up, and quality coordination for export business.

Company Snapshot

Qingdao Best-Integrity Trade Co., Ltd. is a garment export company based in Qingdao, China. Our current product categories include outerwear, denim and bottoms, womenswear, pet apparel and accessories, and selected costume styles.

We work with buyers on product development, sampling, pricing, production follow-up, and shipment coordination. Our goal is to support clear communication and efficient order handling from initial inquiry to final delivery.

What to Include in an Inquiry

Useful first-message information

  • Style number, reference image, or product category
  • Estimated quantity or MOQ discussion range
  • Target season, delivery window, or shipment timing
  • Destination market and preferred shipping basis
  • Any known requirements for materials, sizing, labeling, or packaging
